A Special Message For Papa ❤️

Dear Papa,


Life has given me many blessings, but having you as my father is the greatest one. You have always stood behind me, supported my dreams, protected me from difficulties and taught me how to face life with courage. Even when I didn't understand your sacrifices, you continued to work hard for our happiness. I want you to know that I notice everything. I notice your efforts. I notice your care. I notice your love. You are my first teacher, my biggest supporter, and my forever hero. No matter where life takes me, I will always remain your little girl. I love you Papa ❤️


— Your Daughter, Nishtha🥰
